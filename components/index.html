Frozen Yogurt

Healthy, fresh and delicious in taste

April 4

Body Text

The oyya frozen yogurt is pure indulgence. The frozen yogurt is prepared on site at the time of your order with low-fat yogurt and milk. It is a refreshing delicacy with a delicious taste and creaminess. Oyya frozen yogurt is low in fat, with less sugar and packed with probiotics to make it a healthy snack. You can choose from two yogurt flavors every day and from more than twenty toppings such as fresh fruit, chocolate, cookies, nuts and candy. Then you can choose another sauce to complete your oyya frozen yogurt experience!
